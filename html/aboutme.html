About Neymar Jr.

Brazilian Professional Footballer

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior (born 5 February 1992) is a Brazilian forward known for his dribbling, creativity, and flair. He has played for top clubs such as Santos, Barcelona, and Paris Saint-Germain.

Main Skills

  • Dribbling - quick footwork and tricks
  • Speed - explosive pace to beat defenders
  • Finishing - ability to score from any position
  • Passing - assists and vision
  • Free Kicks - accurate set-piece shots

Career Journey

  1. Santos FC (Brazil)
  2. FC Barcelona (Spain)
  3. Paris Saint-Germain (France)
  4. Brazil National Team
